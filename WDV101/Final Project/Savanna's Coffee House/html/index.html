We are St. Paul's Premier Coffee House

Fresh Roasted Coffee

We are St. Paul's coffee shop of choice. We roast our own beans from the best sources around the world. We also offer a range of sandwiches, salads and homemade soups; along with a wide variety of cakes, pastries and desserts. Local entertainment is provided every weekend evening. Welcome to the neighborhood!

Locally Owned and Operated since 2006

Savannah's is a fixture in the local community, where people gather to partake in conversation, art, and good coffee. While the weekends are the shop's busiest times, you will always find someone or something at Savannah's to make you feel like part of the community.
